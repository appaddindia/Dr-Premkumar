Robotic Radical Prostatectomy – Advanced, Precise & Minimally Invasive Prostate Cancer Surgery by Dr. Premkumar K

Robotic Radical Prostatectomy is a state-of-the-art, minimally invasive surgery performed to remove the prostate gland in patients diagnosed with localized prostate cancer. Using the high-precision Da Vinci Robotic System, Dr. Premkumar K ensures superior cancer control, minimal blood loss, faster recovery, and better preservation of urinary continence and sexual function.

  • Why Robotic Surgery? – Enhanced 3D vision, precise instrument control, minimal pain, smaller incisions, and reduced hospital stay compared to open or laparoscopic surgery.

  • Common Indications – Localized or early-stage prostate cancer, rising PSA levels, biopsy-confirmed malignancy, or high-risk lesions requiring complete prostate removal.

  • Preoperative Assessment – PSA test, MRI prostate, biopsy evaluation, staging scans, and functional assessment to determine suitability for robotic surgery.

  • Surgical Advantages – Precise tumor removal, nerve-sparing capability for preserving erections, minimal bleeding, reduced postoperative pain, and excellent cosmetic results.

  • Procedure Overview – Through small keyhole incisions, robotic instruments remove the prostate gland, seminal vesicles, and nearby tissues while protecting nerves and urinary structures.

  • Postoperative Recovery – Faster mobilization, shorter hospital stay, quick return to normal activities, reduced catheter duration, and improved healing time.

  • Functional Outcomes – Better urinary continence recovery, higher precision for cancer clearance, and improved sexual function preservation with nerve-sparing techniques.

  • Possible Side Effects Managed – Temporary urinary leakage, erectile dysfunction, lymphocele formation, or mild pain — all minimized with robotic expertise and structured rehabilitation.

  • Long-Term Follow-Up – PSA monitoring, imaging when required, urinary function assessment, and cancer surveillance to ensure long-term cure and quality of life.

With advanced robotic technology and specialized expertise, Dr. Premkumar K delivers exceptional outcomes in prostate cancer surgery, ensuring safety, precision, and faster recovery.

If you or a loved one has been diagnosed with prostate cancer, consult Dr. Premkumar K for expert evaluation and advanced Robotic Radical Prostatectomy treatment.
